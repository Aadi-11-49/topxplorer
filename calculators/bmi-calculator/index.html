BMI Calculator

Calculate your Body Mass Index (BMI) and view detailed health stats.

What is a BMI Calculator?

A BMI calculator is an online tool used to calculate Body Mass Index based on a person's height and weight. Body Mass Index is a widely used measurement that helps assess whether a person is underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.

How Does the BMI Calculator Work?

This BMI calculator uses a standard mathematical formula that divides your weight in kilograms by the square of your height in meters. The resulting value is your BMI score, which is then classified into different health categories.

The calculator also displays visual BMI statistics, making it easier to understand where your BMI falls within the healthy range.

Why is BMI Important?

Body Mass Index is commonly used by healthcare professionals to assess potential health risks related to body weight. A BMI outside the normal range may indicate higher risks of conditions such as heart disease, diabetes, and high blood pressure.

This BMI calculator is designed for informational purposes only and should not replace professional medical advice.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this BMI calculator accurate?

Yes, this BMI calculator uses the standard BMI formula and provides accurate results for adults.

Does BMI apply to children?

BMI calculations for children and teenagers differ and require age and gender-specific charts. This calculator is intended for adults.

Can BMI determine overall health?

BMI is a general indicator and does not measure body fat directly. Factors such as muscle mass, age, and lifestyle also affect overall health.

Is my height and weight data stored?

No, all calculations are done locally in your browser and no personal data is stored or shared.
